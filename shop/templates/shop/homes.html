Abraca Data

We have been facing an issue in choosing the right restaurant for the right item while ordering on Zomato. Although Zomato has its own Restaurant viz Rating System, It’s a tedious task to go through each restaurant and have a look at ratings and price for each item. What if I tell you, you’ll be able to select the best restaurant for your favourite dishes in fractions of seconds, no matter where you are. This inspires us to make a project which analyzes the Zomato Food Delivery. Nothing is more important than time in today’s world. Time is Precious. Hurray! We’ll save your hours and hours, which generally you used to go through review

Objectives

We are performing food analysis of Zomato: The food delivery app ,so that people can easily select the nearby restaurants which are most suitable and are affordable for them. We will be performing a Data Visualization, which will help End Users to do their task easily and rapidly. 2 They can select the cuisine according to their choice by viewing the ratings. We will predict the timings which are most preferred while ordering the food from Zomato. Customers do not need to ask anyone about the ratings and the food quality, they can check all of it within a few seconds on their own. We will also analyze which category of people are more likely to order the food from Zomato.
